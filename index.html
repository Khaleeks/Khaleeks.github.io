about the film

It all started with a group of friends laughing about how overwhelming the first weeks of college can be. We all had the same struggles—getting lost on campus, questioning if we were doing things right, and trying to stay "very demure, very mindful" just to fit in. That phrase became our inside joke and eventually the heart of our film. We wanted to capture those moments of uncertainty, growth, and laughter, and share them with you. The NYUAD Freshman 2024 Guide is more than just a campus tour; it’s our personal story. We’ve been there, wondering if we’d find our place, and now we’re here to show you around—not just the campus, but the small corners where we found comfort and connection. This film, built around the viral trend, reflects our journey and is our way of saying, "You’re not alone, and you belong here." Welcome to NYUAD.

Cast

Khaleeqa

Khaleeqa is more than just the junior who's "been there, done that"—she's the friend who knows exactly how overwhelming it can feel when you first step onto campus. With her warm smile and calm presence, she’s the big sister you didn’t know you needed, gently guiding you through the chaos. Having survived her own freshman struggles, Khaleeqa's part in this film isn’t just about giving a tour—it’s about passing on the little pieces of advice and comfort she wishes she’d had when she first arrived at NYUAD.

Asma

Asma is every bit the wide-eyed freshman, still figuring things out but determined to make the most of every moment. She’s the kind of person who asks the questions everyone else is afraid to ask, bringing a fresh curiosity that reminds us of what it’s like to be new. Her role in the film captures that feeling of stepping into something big and unknown, yet being willing to laugh at the small missteps along the way. Asma’s journey is a reminder that being “very demure, very mindful” is fine, but it’s okay to be yourself, too.

Tiffany

Tiffany is the one who dives headfirst into new experiences, not afraid to get lost or take a wrong turn. She’s the adventurous freshman, always ready to laugh at herself and embrace the unexpected. In the film, Tiffany is the energy that pulls everyone together—the one who makes even the awkward moments fun. Her bright spirit shows that college is about more than just finding classrooms—it’s about finding your own way, in your own time, with a few good friends by your side.
